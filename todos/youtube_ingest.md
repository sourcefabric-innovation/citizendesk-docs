
**Notions on enabling Youtube ingest**

The _url-ingest_ could be extended to take specific data on recognized URL type, e.g. on Youtube videos.
We already followed such an approach during development of the [bookmarklet](https://github.com/sourcefabric-innovation/citizendesk-ubiquitous) for grabbing online data.

The part that is specific to Youtube is at
[video dealing](https://github.com/sourcefabric-innovation/citizendesk-ubiquitous/blob/master/mini/front/lets/ubi_cd_sites_video.js)
there.
